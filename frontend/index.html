How to Use the Accessify

1. Search for a Location

Type the place you want to go (e.g., library, canteen, department) into the search bar.

2. Select Accessibility Needs

Choose the accessibility features that matter to you:

  • Ramp
  • Elevator
  • Accessible Washroom
  • Prayer Area
  • Lactating Area

Click Search after selecting your needs.

3. View Map Results

Pins will appear only for locations that match your selected criteria.
Each pin is color-coded based on accessibility:

  • 🟢 Green → Highly accessible
  • 🟡 Yellow → Moderately accessible
  • 🔴 Red → Poor accessibility

4. Check Accessibility Details

Click on a pin to open its accessibility card, which shows:

  • Criteria Met: e.g., 2 out of 3 selected features are available
  • Personalized Score: based only on your selected needs
  • Service Breakdown: scores for each selected service
  • Overall Score and Score breakdown for all services
  • Photos: real-world images for verification

5. Submit a Review

  • Scroll to the end of the card and click Add Review to open the review form.
  • Rate each service from 1 (lowest) to 5 (highest).
  • If you don’t want to rate a service, leave it blank.
  • Upload at least one photo (required) as proof of accessibility.

6. Review Impact

  • Your review updates the accessibility scores.
  • Uploaded photos help others verify conditions.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • At least one image is required when submitting a review.
  • Ratings are optional per service, but images are mandatory.
  • Scores are dynamically updated based on user feedback.
